About Me


Zahra is my legal name but I go by "Yalda". I studied in National Organization for Development of Exceptional Talents in Iran for middle school and high school. I gained experience with robotics coding which inspired my passion for coding and technology. I then studied Electrical Engineering for two years in QIAU and worked more on mechanics of robotics in projects. After moving to Seattle in 2014, I started school with more focus on entering healthcare as my newfound passion and worked full-time between two jobs as a Pharmacy Technician and a Math Tutor. I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Biochemistry from University of Washington, Seattle in 2018.
For the past two years I was content with my passion but I felt like something was missing and so I wanted to go back to programming so I entered and finished a full stack web development bootcamp with certification from University of Washington. Thanks to my knowledge I am able to create more user friendly applications especially with my good eye in design as I enjoy creative problem solving and collaborative teamwork.

I love volleyball, hiking, meditation and traveling. At some point in the future, it would be a great advantage to work remotely and travel and start the life I have put on hold.
